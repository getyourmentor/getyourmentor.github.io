In today's world where along with technical skills, people also require to be good with a certain non technical aspects, it becomes tough for you to learn so many things. A lot of times, you might wish to learn along with someone or have someone who is good at a particular field to guide you so that you know what's necessary to learn and what's not.

Get Your Mentor helps you form groups and find mentors to learn about various topics and find the right path to go about learning a particular topic. Members in your study group might not necessarily be from your same city or college, so this also increases your chances to know more people around the world, thereby helping you expand your network.

Why Get Your Mentor?

Having a study group and a guide or a mentor would help you learn more efficiently and quickly as it would help you :
  • Maintain the competitive spirit
  • Not feel alone while learning
  • Have a group with whom you can discuss about various doubts one might get
  • Lastly, learn only what actually is necessary for you!
